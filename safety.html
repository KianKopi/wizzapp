Safety Hub

Your safety is our priority

Our Safety Approach

At Wizz, we take safety seriously across all our platforms. We've implemented comprehensive measures to protect our users on both our film watch party service and Wizz.live social networking app, maintaining a secure environment for everyone.

Safety First

Zero Tolerance for Harm

We don't allow violence, threats, hate speech, scams, or anything that puts people at risk. Content that's graphic, discriminatory, or exploitative is also a no-go.

Reporting & Moderation

If something feels off — like a fake profile, a user asking for money, or content that crosses a line — report it immediately. Our Safety team works around the clock to review individual cases and act fast.

Protecting Your Privacy

  • Be careful about publicly posting personal details like your full name, home address, or phone number
  • Keep private photos and videos private
  • Never share sensitive information with people you don't know
  • Sharing someone else's personal information without consent is strictly off-limits

Respect Others

  • No bullying, harassment or any behavior meant to embarrass or hurt someone
  • No spreading personal rumors or making comments that punch down
  • No sexual harassment and unwanted advances in any form
  • If someone makes you uncomfortable, block and report them right away

Safety Tools

Wizz provides several tools to help you stay safe:

  • Block & Report: Block users and report inappropriate content
  • Privacy Settings: Control who can contact you and see your information
  • Two-Factor Authentication: Add an extra layer of security to your account
  • Parental Controls: Parents can set up controls to manage content access

Safety Tips

  • Never share passwords or personal information
  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Be cautious about who you invite to private rooms
  • Report suspicious activity immediately
  • Trust your instincts — if something feels wrong, it probably is
  • Keep your device software up to date
