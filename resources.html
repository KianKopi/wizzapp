Resources and Support

We provide comprehensive resources to help you get the most out of Wizz and stay safe while using our platform. Whether you're new to Wizz or an experienced user, you'll find helpful guides, tutorials, and safety information here.

1. Getting Started Guides

New to Wizz? Start with our beginner guides to learn the basics:

  • Creating Your Account: Step-by-step guide to setting up your Wizz profile
  • Joining Rooms: Learn how to find and join watch parties
  • Creating Your Own Room: Host your own watch party and invite friends
  • Connecting Streaming Services: Link your Netflix, Disney+, and other subscriptions
  • Customizing Your Profile: Personalize your Wizz experience

2. Safety Resources

Your safety is our priority. We provide resources to help you use Wizz safely:

  • Community Guidelines: Understand our rules and expectations
  • Reporting Violations: How to report inappropriate behavior or content
  • Privacy Settings: Control who can contact you and see your information
  • Blocking and Muting: Manage interactions with other users
  • Recognizing Scams: Learn how to identify and avoid common scams

3. Technical Support

Having technical issues? We have resources to help:

  • Troubleshooting Guide: Solutions to common technical problems
  • System Requirements: Ensure your device meets minimum requirements
  • Connection Issues: Optimize your internet connection for better performance
  • Audio and Video Problems: Fix streaming quality issues
  • Browser Compatibility: Recommended browsers for best experience

4. Community Resources

Connect with the Wizz community:

  • Community Forum: Discuss with other users and share experiences
  • Social Media: Follow us on Instagram, Twitter, TikTok, and YouTube
  • Blog: Read articles about entertainment, community stories, and updates
  • Events: Participate in community events and watch parties
  • Feedback: Share your ideas and suggestions for improvements

5. Accessibility Resources

We're committed to making Wizz accessible to everyone:

  • Keyboard Navigation: Use Wizz without a mouse
  • Screen Reader Support: Compatible with popular screen readers
  • Closed Captions: Available for supported content
  • High Contrast Mode: Easier viewing for users with visual impairments
  • Accessibility Settings: Customize your experience

Find answers to common questions in our FAQ section. Topics include account management, streaming, safety, and technical issues.
