How we use cookies to enhance your experience

Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how Wizz uses cookies and similar tracking technologies. By using Wizz, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. For more information about how we handle your data, please see our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They contain information about your browsing activity and preferences. Cookies help websites remember information about you and provide a better user experience.

Similar technologies include web beacons, pixels, and local storage, which serve similar purposes.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas. Without these cookies, the website cannot operate correctly.

  • Session cookies for maintaining your login
  • Security cookies for fraud prevention
  • Preference cookies for remembering your choices

Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how you use our website, such as which pages you visit and how long you spend on them. This helps us understand user behavior and improve our services.

  • Analytics cookies to track page views and user interactions
  • Performance monitoring cookies to identify errors

Functional Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences and settings to provide a personalized experience.

  • Language preference cookies
  • Theme preference cookies
  • User preference cookies

Marketing Cookies

These cookies track your activity across websites to deliver targeted advertising and measure campaign effectiveness.

  • Advertising cookies from third-party partners
  • Retargeting cookies for personalized ads

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party service providers to place cookies on your device for analytics, advertising, and other purposes. These third parties include:

  • Google Analytics for website analytics
  • Advertising networks for targeted ads
  • Social media platforms for integration

These third parties have their own cookie policies and privacy practices. We recommend reviewing their policies for more information.

4. How Long Do Cookies Last?

Cookies have different lifespans:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a specified period (days, months, or years)

5. Your Cookie Choices

You have control over cookies on your device:

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through settings. You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject all cookies
  • Accept only certain types of cookies
  • Delete existing cookies

Note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

Cookie Consent

When you first visit Wizz, you'll see a cookie consent banner. You can choose which types of cookies to accept.

Opt-Out Options

You can opt out of targeted advertising through industry opt-out tools and your browser's privacy settings.

6. Do Not Track

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" feature. Currently, there is no industry standard for recognizing Do Not Track signals, so Wizz does not respond to these signals. However, you can use other tools to control cookies as described above.

7. Data Security

We take the security of cookie data seriously. All cookies are transmitted over secure connections and stored securely. We do not store sensitive personal information in cookies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of Wizz following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

If you have questions about our use of cookies or this policy, please contact us:
