Your Rights Over Your Data

Understanding your privacy rights

Your Rights Over Your Data

At Wizz, we believe you have fundamental rights over your personal data. This page explains your rights and how to exercise them. For more information about how we collect and use your data, please see our Privacy Policy.

1. Right to Access

You have the right to access your personal data that we hold. You can request a copy of all your data at any time.

How to exercise this right: Contact us at privacy@wizzapp.live with your request. We will provide your data in a portable format within 30 days.

2. Right to Rectification

You have the right to correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data. If you believe any of your information is incorrect, you can request that we update it.

How to exercise this right: Log into your account and update your profile information directly, or contact us at privacy@wizzapp.live to request corrections.

3. Right to Erasure

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances. This is sometimes called the "right to be forgotten."

How to exercise this right: Contact us at privacy@wizzapp.live with your deletion request. We will delete your data within 30 days, except where we have a legal obligation to retain it.

4. Right to Restrict Processing

You have the right to request that we limit how we use your personal data in certain situations, such as while we verify the accuracy of disputed data.

How to exercise this right: Contact us at privacy@wizzapp.live to request restrictions on how we process your data.

5. Right to Data Portability

You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format, and to transmit it to another service provider.

How to exercise this right: Contact us at privacy@wizzapp.live to request your data in a portable format. We will provide it within 30 days.

6. Right to Object

You have the right to object to certain types of data processing, including marketing communications and profiling.

How to exercise this right: You can unsubscribe from marketing emails using the link in any email, or contact us at privacy@wizzapp.live to object to other types of processing.

7. Right to Withdraw Consent

If we process your data based on your consent, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time.

How to exercise this right: Contact us at privacy@wizzapp.live to withdraw your consent. This will not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

8. Right to Lodge a Complaint

If you believe we have violated your data protection rights, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

How to exercise this right: Contact your local data protection authority. You can also contact us first at privacy@wizzapp.live to attempt to resolve the issue.

9. Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us with the following information:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address associated with your Wizz account
  • A description of your request
  • Any supporting documentation

We will respond to your request within 30 days. If your request is complex, we may extend this period by up to two additional months.

10. No Discrimination

We will not discriminate against you for exercising your data protection rights. You will continue to have access to all Wizz services and features regardless of whether you exercise these rights.

11. Contact Information

For questions about your data rights or to exercise any of these rights, please contact us:

Email: privacy@wizzapp.live

General Support: support@wizzapp.live
